What is PTE Academic?
The Pearson Test of English Academic (PTE Academic) is a computer-based English language proficiency test designed for non-native English speakers who wish to study, work, or migrate to English-speaking countries. Recognized by thousands of educational institutions, governments for education and migration.
Duration: Approximately 2 hours
Scoring: Scale of 10 to 90
Results available within 48 hours
Integrated scoring system allows you to gain points from different modules
100% computer-based test, taken in a test center
Evaluates your academic English

- Summarize Written Text: Summarize a given text in one sentence.

Frequently Asked Questions
How can I book the PTE Academic exam?
[https://www.pearsonpte.com/](https://www.pearsonpte.com/)
What is the test fee for the PTE Academic exam?
The cost of the PTE Academic varies depending on your country. Typically, it ranges from $180 to $220 USD. Some locations may also charge additional fees for late registration or rescheduling.
How long are PTE Academic scores valid?
PTE Academic scores are valid for two years from the date of the exam.
Can I reschedule or cancel my PTE Academic test?

### What Our Star Students Say

You can reschedule your PTE test for free if done more than 14 days before the test, with a maximum of six reschedules allowed. If less than 14 days remain, rescheduling is not possible, and you must cancel and book a new test, which may incur a fee. Cancellation provides a full refund if done at least 14 days before the test, a 50% refund between 8-13 days, and no refund within 7 days. Pearson reserves the right to cancel or reschedule tests, offering a refund or new test date. In cases of illness or family emergencies, you may request a free reschedule by submitting evidence, but approval is at Pearson’s discretion.
Can I take the PTE Academic at home?
Yes, you can take the PTE Academic Online version, which allows you to take the test from home, provided you meet the technical and environmental requirements. However, the online version may not be accepted for visa applications.
What type of ID is accepted on PTE exam day?
For the PTE Academic exam, a valid, government-issued passport is the primary and most commonly accepted form of identification. It must be presented at the test center or during the online test check-in process. In some countries, other forms of government-issued ID, such as a national ID card, may be accepted, but this is rare and depends on local regulations. Always check the specific ID requirements for your test location when booking your exam to ensure your ID will be accepted.
